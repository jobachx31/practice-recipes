Waffle

Description

A waffle is a dish made from leavened batter or dough that is cooked between two plates that are patterned to give a characteristic size, shape, and surface impression.

Ingredients

  • eggs
  • baking powder
  • flour
  • sugar
  • butter
  • salt
  • milk

Steps

  1. Preheat iron to desired temperature.
  2. Mix flour, salt, baking powder, and sugar
  3. Beat eggs, stir in milk and batter.
  4. Laddle into waffle iron, cook till golden brown.
